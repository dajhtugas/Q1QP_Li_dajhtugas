BACKGROUND OF THE STUDY



Depression cases have been increasing in today’s society.
This could negatively affect our present and the future of our society.
With this our group aims to find possible causes
that affect the rate and count of a country’s depression statistic.
This presentation aims to discuss the trends of factors that affect
the rate and count of depression within a country.
