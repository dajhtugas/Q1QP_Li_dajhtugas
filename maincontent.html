In this data, we can see that most countries in the TOP 50 have a Happiness Index Rating of around 101.5.

CONCLUSION:
With the data we have obtained and gathered, we can conclude that Asia is a region where we can find countries with a high prevalance of depression.
Most of the countries that were found in the top 50 came from the Afro-Eurasian Region.
Surprisingly, countries with a high income level have the highest depression prevalence. Factors of these may include: pressure from legacy
and being the breadwinner of the family.
